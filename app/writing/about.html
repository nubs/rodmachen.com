About

Rod Machen

Rod is a freelance writer based in Austin, TX, focusing on food, television, books and the arts. He started his career in journalism but now works in education.

After being raised in the Texas Panhandle, Rod spent his college years at Texas A&M. He first moved to Austin in 1998. In 2002, he moved to Seattle where he lived for several years and drank innumerable cups of coffee. In 2007, he moved back and now spends his time raising two little girls and trying to have as interesting a time as possible.
